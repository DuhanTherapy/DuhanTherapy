Introduction

I am an experienced Person- centred psychotherapist and an individual member of British Association of Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP), bound by its code of ethics also commitment to ongoing professional development. I am offering confidential, down to earth counselling on a short and long term basis via Zoom video conference or face to face. Working both an English and Turkish with individuals and couples. My practice follows the person -centred approach, where you will receive the core conditions to reach your full potential. It is an approach to put you at the center of our therapeutic counselling journey, empower you. Instead of telling you what to do. I work in a way that places emphasis on the therapeutic relationship through awareness, sensitivity, non-judgement, respect and empathy. Mindfulness both underpins my approach and may also incorporated into therapy if appropriate.

Duhan Therapy

I shall neither give you an advice or trying to "fix things" for you but actively listen to your difficulties and help you to clarify your thoughts, feelings and emotions in a safe, non-judgmental and supportive environment. We may look at unresolved past issues, your current life situation, the future concerns and managing your life. The aim of our therapeutic work together is to help you gain a greater awareness, understanding, feel more control of your life and open yourself to all of your possibilities and potentials.
